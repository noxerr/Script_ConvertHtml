Slab: Subtract Boundary

Keyboard:
vaSlabSubtract
Icon:
left click
Slabs toolbar:
Menu:
VisualARQ > Slab > Subtract Boundary

Option to subtract a boundary from a slab.

When you run the vaSlabSubtract command, follow the steps from the command line. Press the right mouse button or ENTER to accept and go to the next step.

Steps:

  1. Select the slab you wish to subtract the boundary from.
  2. Select the boundary you wish to subtract.

The slab subtracts the silhouette of the new boundary from its shape.

NOTE: Boundaries do not need to be in the slab plane, but they must be planar and closed curves.
