Roof: Subtract Boundary

Keyboard:
vaRoofSubtract
Icon:
left click
Roofs toolbar:
Menu:
VisualARQ > Roof > Subtract Boundary

Option to subtract a boundary from a roof.

When you run the vaRoofSubtract command, follow the steps from the command line. Press the right mouse button or ENTER to accept and go to the next step.

Steps:

  1. Select the roof you wish to subtract the boundary from.
  2. Select the boundary you wish to subtract.

The roof subtracts the silhouette of the new boundary from its shape.

NOTE: Boundaries do not need to be in the roof plane, but they must be closed and planar curves.
