Wall: Reverse

Keyboard:
vaWallReverse
Icon:
left click
Walls toolbar:
Menu:
VisualARQ > Wall > Reverse

Command to reverse the direction of a wall's path and sides: the start point becomes the final point, the inner part becomes the outer part, and vice versa.

NOTE: Take into account that all doors, windows and openings included in the selected wall keep their position, but they may change their opening side when you reverse the wall path.
